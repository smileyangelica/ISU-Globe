Terminal Display

Clean ASCII sphere in your terminal. Colors show depth and landmass. Updates 20 times per second for smooth animation.

Rotation Behavior

Continuous smooth rotation around vertical axis (Y-axis in 3D space). Complete revolution takes about 42 seconds.
